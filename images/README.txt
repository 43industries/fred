HOW TO ADD PHOTOS
================

Put your photos in this folder (fred/images/) with these exact names:

  photo1.jpg - "The First Glance"
  photo2.jpg - "Our First Adventure"
  photo3.jpg - "Laughter and Joy"
  photo4.jpg - "Quiet Moments"
  photo5.jpg - "Our Love Grows"
  photo6.jpg - "Forever and Always"

Supported formats: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .gif, .webp

If you use a different format (e.g. .png), update the filename in index.html 
(e.g. change "photo1.jpg" to "photo1.png").

Until you add photos, the website will show a placeholder with a heart icon.
